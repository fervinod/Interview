## Meta-Specific Themes

Meta interviewers often probe around:

* “Move fast”
* Bias toward action
* Handling ambiguity
* Data-driven thinking
* User impact
* Learning quickly
* Operating at scale
* Cross-functional collaboration

You’ll usually want to answer using the STAR format:

* Situation
* Task
* Action
* Result

A strong Meta behavioral answer usually includes:

* Clear metrics/results
* Specific actions YOU took
* Fast decision-making
* Tradeoffs considered
* Reflection/learning

Example structure:

> “The issue was causing 18% checkout failure. I coordinated with infra and backend teams, proposed a rollback strategy, implemented monitoring, and reduced failures to under 1% within 24 hours.”
